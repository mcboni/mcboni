Sarman Integrated Farms and Solutions LTD

The Sarman Group was created in August 2005 with the objective of processing of agricultural produce, agricultural commodities and plastics packaging. The company process oil seeds into high quality edible oil and its derivatives to meet the expanding needs of the rapidly growing market in Nigeria. The company later expanded in to a full scale agricultural operation with investments in poultry, fish, dairy, cultivation fruits and vegetables and food production (flour & edible oil). The company have its own fish and chicken hatcheries and full-scale animal feed production line for its own consumption and sells off excess to other farmers.
